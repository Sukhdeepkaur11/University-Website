Our Courses

Accounting

The Accounting major is designed to provide students with the general, business
and accounting knowledge necessary for them to succeed as professional accountants.

  • Four year degree
  • Full-time program
  • You can enter this program directly from high school
  • You can begin this program>off-campus

Careers

Students who major in accounting may continue their professional development with careers in the following areas:

  • accountant
  • auditor
  • budget analyst
  • investment analyst
  • taxation specialist
  • assurance associate

Civil Engineering

We’ve redesigned first-year engineering at USask to help you succeed and make engineering more fun. We call the new first year RE-ENGINEERED. In RE-ENGINEERED, Daily tutorials are part of the schedule so you can get help when you need it. You will choose your engineering specialty – known as your major, or discipline – at the end of your first year. /p>

  • Four year degree
  • Full-time program
  • You can enter this program directly from high school
  • You can begin this program>off-campus

Careers

  • public and private sector
  • municipal (urban or rural), provincial and federal governments
  • construction
  • resource development (for example, mining)
  • project management
