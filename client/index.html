World's Biggest University

The Eduford University is western Canada’s first university, Manitoba's only research-intensive university, is located on original lands of Anishinaabeg,
Cree, Oji-Cree, Dakota, and Dene peoples, and on the homeland of the Métis Nation.

World Class Library

Archives and Special Collections comprise both physical and digitized content. Contact the archives for assistance with your research and with your request for access to physical collections.

Largest Play Ground

This park features two tennis courts, one baseball diamond and backstop, playground with slide, baby swings, a Rich Beyer sculpture of a sasquatch, adult exercise equipment and bathroom facilities.

Tasty and Healthy Food

On campus we provide facilities such as a bar and canteen for our students and visitors. The canteen offers a wide range of dishes, freshly cooked and prepared, and with attention paid to quality.
